About Me

I am a passionate Programmer

I’m a first-year student at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW), pursuing a B.Tech degree in computer science. I’m focused in building a strong foundation in engineering and technology, which I hope will open doors to various career opportunities in the future.
I’m particularly interested in web development, data science and machine learning and I’m excited to explore these fields more deeply through my coursework and projects. Outside of academics, I plan to get involved in campus activities, join relevant clubs or societies, and seek internships to gain practical experience.
In the long term, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the tech industry by working on innovative projects and solving real-world problems. I’m eager to learn, grow, and make the most of the opportunities that come my way .
